Insect Extinction Risks Our Own Survival

Insects are the "lever-pullers of the world", providing a range of services vital to human survival. They pollinate our crops, recycle our waste and return nutrients to the soil. Alarmingly, scientific research has shown that insect populations are plummeting across the world, putting our own survival at risk.

Insects not only provide important services, they can also be visually stunning.
